# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In Short

- **Be kind.** Assume contributors are acting in good faith.
- **Focus on resources, not people.** Disagree with a link's value, not the person who suggested it.
- **No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks** — in issues, PRs, or anywhere this project's name is attached.
- **Maintainers will remove content that violates this** and may block repeat offenders.

## Reporting

Open an issue marked `[CoC]`, or contact the maintainer directly via the email listed on their GitHub profile.
