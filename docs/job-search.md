# 💼 Job Search Platforms

Job boards and AI-assisted application tools, especially for AI/ML and Data roles.

## Job Boards

- **[Wellfound (formerly AngelList)](https://wellfound.com/jobs)** — Best startup-job board.
- **[Himalayas](https://himalayas.app/)** — Remote-first companies; clean filtering.
- **[New Grad Jobs](https://www.newgrad-jobs.com/)** — Entry-level + new-grad listings.
- **[The Muse](https://www.themuse.com/)** — Jobs + company culture pages.
- **[The Anti Job Board](https://www.theantijobboard.com/)** — Curated alternative listings.
- **[Skillsire](https://www.skillsire.com/jobs)** — Tech-focused board.
- **[Pave Careers](https://www.pave.com/company/careers)** — Compensation-platform jobs (and the salary data is itself useful).

## AI-Assisted Application Tools

- **[JobCopilot](https://app.jobcopilot.com/)** — AI-assisted job applications.
- **[SpeedyApply](https://www.speedyapply.com/)** — Bulk application automation.
- **[SimpleApply](https://simpleapply.ai/)** — AI application assistance.

## Career Coaching & Strategy

- **[Cultivated Culture](https://cultivatedculture.com/)** — Resume/job-search strategy resources.
- **[Job Hunt Masterclass (Nistha Tripathi)](https://courses.scholarstrategy.com/courses/Internship-Jobhunt-Masterclass-Nistha-Tripathi)** — Structured job-hunt course.

## Remote-Specific

- **[Hiring Remote List](https://github.com/yashikabadaya/Hiring-Remote)** — Curated remote-friendly company list.

## Org Research

- **[The Org](https://theorg.com/)** — Company org charts; useful for cold outreach and identifying hiring managers.
